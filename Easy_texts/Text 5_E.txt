Ways to eat more high fibre foods
Eat wholemeal bread, granary or high fibre white bread. Eat high fibre breakfast cereals e.g. Shredded Wheat, Weetabix, Branflakes, Allbran, porridge. Choose wholegrain crackers, crispbreads and biscuits. Include beans and pulses e.g. baked beans, lentil soup, beans in stews, casseroles and salads. Have at least 5 portions of fruit and vegetables each day.
Remember:
• increase the amount of high fibre foods slowly
• have at least 8 drinks each day e.g. water, tea, coffee, milk, squash, fruit juice
