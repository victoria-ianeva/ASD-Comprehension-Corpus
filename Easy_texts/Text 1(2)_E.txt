1.	Professionals need to know more and have better plans
Professionals working with children or young people on the spectrum need to know more about the different ways to communicate with that child or young person.
Professionals want:
• To know more about autism and have good advice and training.
• To know more about how to help children who need more support.
• To be able to use specialist services - like speech and language therapists and occupational therapists.
