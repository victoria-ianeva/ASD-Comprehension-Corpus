1.	Day to Day
Did you know you can improve your fitness simply by being more active in your day to day life?
Here are some ideas………...
·	Walk to the shops
·	Do not’ use the phone, walk to talk to a neighbour.
·	Use the stairs, not the lift or escalator.
·	Get off the bus a stop early and walk home
·	When family and friends come to visit go for a walk together before having a cup of tea
